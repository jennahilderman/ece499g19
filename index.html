Background

The project addresses the need for efficient solar energy systems for sustainable energy solutions and off-grid living. Maximum Power Point Tracking (MPPT) solar charge controllers are crucial for maximizing the efficiency of solar panels, significantly improving solar energy conversion by 30-40% over other designs.

Design

Our design involves building an MPPT solar charge controller that works with a solar panel or array of panels to charge a battery. The MPPT controller continuously tracks the maximum power point of the solar panels and adjusts the PWM duty cycle of the synchronous buck converter to ensure optimal voltage and efficient battery charging.

Results/Findings

  • Successful implementation of the MPPT algorithm
  • Optimized energy extraction from solar panels under varying conditions
  • Efficient charging of a 12V lead-acid battery
  • Development of a custom PCB for the MPPT controller
